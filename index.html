Welcome to Cardboardwater.

This project was created in 48 hours for the C4KLA Hackathon held from October 2 to October 4, 2015. It was created as a response to a challenge by World Vision to inspire people to join the Clean Water cause through the use of Google cardboard. If you were inspired by this project, please donate to bring clean water to those who need it at http://www.worldvision.org/our-impact/clean-water.
